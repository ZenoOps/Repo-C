Quick Declines 
Objective 
To document the pre-defined quick decline rules 
Criteria for Quick Declines 
No Quick Decline Business 
Rule Comms 
1 Backdating - Inception date is 
before Quote Date 
Decline Email to be sent: 
“Thank you for your quotation submission for the above-
mentioned customer. 
 We advise that Zurich will not be offering a quotation in 
this instance due to the following reason: 
The policy inception date is prior to the current date. 
Thank you for the opportunity to review and we look 
forward to considering other opportunities in the future. 
Should you like to further discuss, please contact your 
local Zurich property underwriter.” 
2 Total Declared Values < $5M 
Decline Email to be sent: 
“Thank you for your quotation submission for the above-
mentioned customer. 
We advise that Zurich will not be offering a quotation in 
this instance due to the following reason: 
The combined Declared Values for Section(s) 1 and 2 is 
below our required minimum values for this product.
