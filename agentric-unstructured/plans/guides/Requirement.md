# Hackathon step

1. Broker submission received via email
   Pre-processed anonymized submission data provided. IRL extraction of structured and unstructured data from the submission document.
2. High level information created in CRM system to keep track of opportunity
   Create an entry in database of your choice, recording the customer name, customer address, LoB, effectivity date, submission status.

- Nice to have: check for clashes, i.e. the submission is a duplicate.

3. Triage the opportunity and assess whether it is in line with Zurich risk appetite
   Compare the information in the submission to the (mock) underwriting guidelines.
   a. Missing information
   Where there is information missing, draft an email following the provided email communication templates.
   b. Decline
   Where not in appetite, draft an email declining the business to the broker following the provided email guidelines.
   Document the judgement in the database from step 2.
   c. In appetite= proceed to underwriting process
   Compare the risk to the provided (mock) underwriting guidelines.
   Document the judgement why the risk is in appetite in the database from step 2.
4. Structure data
   Extract relevant data from the submission and structure according to the template provided in new (underwriting database)
5. Create underwriting narrative, why the risk is worth taking and the rationale behind it
   Using the structured information and the risk scores generated, create an Underwriting Narrative that provides rationale/justification on the decision.
